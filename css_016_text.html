UK To Invite North Korea For Queen Elizabeth's Funeral: Report Queen Elizabeth II's funeral is scheduled to take place on September 19. (File) London: Britain is inviting a representative from North Korea to attend Queen Elizabeth's funeral on Monday, but Afghanistan, Syria and Venezuela will not be given an invite, a foreign office source said on Wednesday. The invitation for North Korea would be at an ambassadorial level, the source said. Queen Elizabeth's funeral will take place in London on September 19, and a host of world leaders, royalty and other dignitaries have already said they will attend.
